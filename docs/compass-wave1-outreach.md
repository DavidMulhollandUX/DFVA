## Running the interviews (carry from the Validate pack)

- **Open every one:** *"I'm not here to sell anything — I'm trying to find the reasons this shouldn't exist. Treat it as a deliberate attempt to talk me out of it."*
- **The artifact-reaction move:** don't ask "would you want this?" — put the artifact down and watch what they *do*. Reach for a decision? Ask to see more? Deflect? That behaviour is the signal (backlog §artifact tests).
- **Close every one:** *"What didn't I ask?"* · *"Who else should I speak to?"* (feeds the 22-list) · *"Would you bet against us — why?"*
- **Capture immediately** using the block in [compass-validate.md](compass-validate.md#capturing-insight) — note especially the *invalidated/challenged* column and which hypothesis moved.
- **Decision rule:** after ~4–5 interviews, reassess — COMPASS survives if H1 *and* H2 hold and at least one of H3/H4 holds. H4 is already de-risked on the desk side ([EXP-02](compass-exp02-gatekeeper-evidence.md)).
