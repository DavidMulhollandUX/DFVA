The twin gap is fixable
AI Literacy sits at 1/3 in ten of 11 programs. Outcome Evidence sits at 1/3 despite real QILT employment data already in the system — a data-folding gap, not an outcomes gap. Closing both is a curriculum decision, not a structural rebuild.
Closest to resilient · the exemplar
M. Climate Science (26) sit 2–2 points from RESILIENT. Master of Data Science is the only program above the AI-literacy floor (2/3) — proof the gap is closable through curriculum.
