Durability as defensible evidence

What it would take for an accreditation panel to treat a curriculum-durability signal as evidence, not vendor opinion — a defined instrument, an honest claim, and a pre-registered measurement programme that validates the score against graduate outcomes. Worked against Master of Information Systems (18/36, HIGH RISK).

2 · The evidence base (stated honestly)

The mechanism is evidenced; the instrument is the open question

"No causal evidence links labour-market-informed curriculum revision to graduate outcomes."
Demand-aligned curriculum revision is now causally evidenced to raise wages (Salomons et al. 2025 — difference-in-differences over 365 German VET updates, ~+3.3% entry wages). What no study yet validates is that a scored durability instrument predicts QILT outcomes for Australian university programs — the evidence COMPASS can be first to produce.
3 · The measurement protocol — PROSPECT-DiD (one staged study, one pre-registration)
0
Pre-register & freeze · months 0–3 · ~free
Lodge a public pre-registration (OSF/AEA) declaring the rubric, the matching protocol (6-digit ASCED field + level + intake), the estimand (ATT, Callaway–Sant'Anna), the primary outcome (GOS full-time employment), and the inference plan. Cryptographically freeze and timestamp baseline scores — this defeats score→outcome leakage and is itself the artefact a panel rewards, available before any outcome lands.
1
Back-test predictive validity · months 3–9 · first publishable signal <1 yr
Under one HREC secondary-use approval, join frozen scores to the institution's own existing GOS/GOS-L unit records at 6-digit ASCED; pool 3–5 cohorts; report calibration, discrimination (AUC) and incremental ΔAUC over a confounder-only baseline. Positive = the score validates; near-zero = a clean, publishable null. Either way is the highest-value result.
2
Embedded difference-in-differences · years 2–7 · confirmatory
Let a staggered-adoption DiD mature as COMPASS-redesigned cohorts reach GOS then GOS-L, reporting event-study pre-trends as they accrue. Upgrade to PLIDA/BLADE ATO-linked verified earnings only once Step 1 shows signal worth scaling.
