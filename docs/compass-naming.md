# COMPASS — Brand Naming: Candidates, Conflict & Domain Due Diligence

**15 master-brand name candidates to replace the generic "COMPASS", with business/trademark-conflict and domain-availability research**

**Prepared by:** David Mulholland, Associate Director, Service Experience & Design, University of Melbourne
**Date:** 2026-06-24
**Builds on:** [Brand Strategy](compass-brand-strategy.md) (§7 flagged "COMPASS"/"DFVA" as un-ownable brand assets) · [Vision](compass-vision.md)

---

> **Why this exists.** The brand strategy (§6–§7) concluded that COMPASS is a *rating agency*, and that for a ratings brand **ownability is the moat** — a signal you cannot trademark and own cannot become *the* standard. "COMPASS" is generic and heavily contested; "DFVA" is unsayable. This document generates ownable alternatives and pressure-tests each against existing businesses, trademarks, and domain availability.

> **What we're naming.** The master brand/company that replaces "COMPASS". Names marked ⊕ also work as the *rating* itself (the Morningstar → "star rating" move): e.g. "a Perdura Rating", "Foreproof-rated".

---

## 1. The candidates

Four creative territories, mined from the strategy: **durability/endurance** (the core proposition "durability, made visible"), **proof/assay/standard** (the rating function), **foresight/leading-signal** (anticipatory), and **navigation** (the only equity worth keeping from "COMPASS"). Optimised for *trust-brand gravitas + modern-SaaS ownability*.

### Invented / made-up

- **Perdura** ⊕ — Latin *perdurare*, "to last all the way through"; live word in ES/IT/PT ("it endures"). The positioning as a single word. *per-DOO-ra.* Warm, substantial, established. Risk: busy namespace (decking/skincare marks).
- **Evidura** ⊕ — coined blend of **evidence + durability**. Encodes two core values at once; seamless, trademarkable. *ev-ih-DURE-ah.* Modern, credible, quietly scientific. Risk: faint pharma feel; one beat to unpack.
- **Prescia** — from *prescience*, knowing in advance; the anticipatory/leading-signal trait. *PRESH-a.* Soft, intelligent, premium-modern. Risk: less distinctive; pharma-adjacent.
- **Veridian** — *veri* (truth) + the *-idian* of **meridian** (navigation bearing) + verdant/lasting echo. Sounds like the Verisk/Experian data-trust family. *veh-RID-ee-an.* Authoritative, institutional.
- **Tenura** — Latin *tenere*, "to hold/keep" (does it *hold* its value?) + echo of academic **tenure** (HE-native). *ten-YOOR-a.* Scholarly, rooted. Risk: "tenure" is politically loaded in universities.

### Compound / portmanteau

- **Foreproof** ⊕ — *fore-* (foresight) + *proof* (proven, durable); "future-proof", sharpened. Instantly decoded by every audience; works as a verb. *FORE-proof.* Confident, plain-spoken.
- **Evermark** ⊕ — *ever* (enduring) + *mark* (the certification mark); points straight at the strategy's endgame (the displayed "COMPASS-rated" badge). *EV-er-mark.* Trustworthy, badge-like.
- **Worthwise** — *worth* (future value) + *wise* (Sage judgment); bridges the buyer and student-facing sides. *WORTH-wise.* Approachable, reassuring. Risk: *-wise* suffix slightly casual.
- **Provalent** — *prove/proven* + *-valent* (value, prevailing strength). *pro-VAL-ent.* Precise, technical. The "safe/average" coinage of the set.

### Metaphorical / evocative

- **Bellwether** — the lead sheep whose bell signals where the flock is heading; *the* finance word for a leading indicator. The conceptual bullseye for "an independent leading signal". *BELL-weth-er.* Erudite, authoritative.
- **Perennial** — lasting and recurring year after year; durability made warm. *per-EN-ee-al.* Premium, evergreen, positive.
- **Ballast** — the weight that keeps a ship upright in a storm; resilience under disruption, with nautical (compass) heritage. *BAL-ast.* Steady, grounded.
- *(Honourable mention: **Touchstone** — the assay stone for testing gold purity = the perfect rating metaphor, but unownable; see DD below.)*

### Short & abstract / modern

- **Lode** ⊕ — from **lodestone**, the magnetic rock that *was* the original compass + "mother lode" (a rich vein of value). Keeps the navigation DNA, sheds the generic word. *lohd.* Spare, modern, confident.
- **Stele** — an inscribed stone monument built to make a record endure and stay visible for millennia; echoes *steel* (strength). *STEEL.* Monumental, artful. Risk: obscure; pronunciation.
- **Datum** — the fixed reference point from which all measurements are taken (the benchmark moat); singular of *data*, without being "Data". *DAY-tum.* Precise, B2B-credible. Risk: cool/clinical.

## 2. Conflict & domain due diligence (researched 2026-06-24)

### Headline findings

1. **Every exact-match `.com` is gone — all 16.** Not one candidate's `.com` is free. The real choice is a **coined name on a clean alt-TLD** or **buying a domain**.
2. **Only the coined names have clean premium alt-TLDs:** Evidura & Provalent (`.ai`/`.io`/`.co` free); Foreproof (`.io`/`.co` free). Every dictionary/Latin word is locked across all TLDs.
3. **Half the list has MAJOR in-sector conflicts — three of them in higher-ed specifically.** Must-drops below.
4. **Bellwether is disqualified** despite the best concept-fit: *Bellwether Education Partners* (US edu research/consulting), a higher-ed *Bellwether Award*, and a live AI-software trademark — a direct multi-front collision in the exact sector.

## 3. Revised Top 5 (conflict + ownability combined)

Ownability is weighted as heavily as concept, per the strategy.

1. **Evidura** — *front-runner.* Coined (best trademark path), open in-sector field (one small legal-evidence app, different vertical, no TM), `.ai`/`.io`/`.co` free today. Homework: vet the `evidura.app` operator and the *Evidera* phonetic proximity.
2. **Foreproof** — cleanest conflict profile of all 15 (nothing, nowhere). Catch: `.com` snapped up 5 weeks ago (parked, not a business) — take `.io`/`.co` or try to buy it. Strongest category-verb potential.
3. **Perdura** — most on-strategy *name*, trademark-workable (all conflicts out-of-class). But every TLD is taken: only pursue on a modified domain (`perduraratings.com`/`tryperdura.com`) **and** after clearing SMARTDECK's AU wordmark.
4. **Provalent** — genuinely available (`.ai`/`.io`/`.co`), open in-sector field. The "safe/forgettable" pick; 3 existing PROVALENT marks to clear. Real, ownable fallback.
5. **Prescia** — workable; `.co` free, parked `.com` buyable. Watch the two **Australian** AI-analytics namesakes (same-jurisdiction adjacency is the live risk).

**Cut:** Bellwether, Veridian, Worthwise, Perennial, Ballast, Datum, Touchstone. **Hold (domain/phonetic problems):** Tenura, Stele, Lode, Evermark.

## 4. Caveat & next steps

This is **open-web due diligence, not legal clearance** — the trademark register back-ends (USPTO TSDR, IP Australia, EUIPO) were largely gated to automated search, so no name here is *cleared*.

Before committing to any name:
1. **Formal trademark clearance** in Nice classes **9 / 35 / 36 / 41 / 42**, across **IP Australia first** (the venture is AU-based), then USPTO (`tmsearch.uspto.gov`) and EUIPO (`TMview`).
2. **Two load-bearing manual checks:** SMARTDECK **"PERDURA"** (IP Australia) and the **`evidura.app`** operator + any Evidura/Evidera registration.
3. **Domain plan:** decide alt-TLD (`.ai` for Evidura/Provalent) vs. a domain purchase (parked `prescia.com`/`foreproof.com`; premium `lode.com`) before finalising.

*Method note: domain status verified via DNS NS-record check + WHOIS (controls passed). Business/trademark conflicts via web research; trademark serial/class numbers are as-reported from USPTO mirrors and must be confirmed at source.*
