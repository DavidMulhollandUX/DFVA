- Australian biomedical research funding competitive but stable; industry R&D modest but growing
- Clinical genomics and precision medicine creating net-new roles
- The 125pt research project is the structural differentiator — demonstrated research capability resists AI substitution
- AI literacy gap: no explicit AI in biomedical research content; computational methods increasingly expected
