**Common patterns across lab-science MSc programs:**
- Research project (100-125pt) is the structural differentiator — graduates with demonstrated research capability resist AI substitution
- PhD pathway is primary for most; industry roles exist but are more modest in Australia
- AI literacy gap is uniform — none have explicit AI content, though computational methods are increasingly present in research
- Lab/field skills create a physical moat against AI that coursework-only programs lack

**Confidence: MEDIUM.** Core patterns consistent across programs. Individual job family signals from Australian Government Labour Market Insights and discipline-specific workforce reports.
