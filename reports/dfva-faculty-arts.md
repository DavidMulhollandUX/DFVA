## 4. DESTINATION CONVERGENCE & EXPOSURE READ

Arts graduates converge on the **public sector and policy** (DFAT, Prime Minister & Cabinet, the ABS, Parliament), the **cultural sector** (NGV, Museums Victoria, ACCA), and media. Modal roles are Research Assistant, Policy Officer and Policy Analyst — analytical, judgement-led work. The highest-volume majors (Psychology n=859, History n=524, Criminology n=513, Politics n=339) are precisely the destination-rich programmes DFVA has not yet assessed.
