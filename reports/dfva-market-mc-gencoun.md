## Key Signals
- Clinical genomics is one of the fastest-growing areas of healthcare. MBS item additions for genomic testing (2025-2026) are driving diagnostic lab expansion and genetic counselling demand.
- Genetic counselling is a regulated profession (HGSA certification) — creates a structural barrier to AI substitution
- The ≥70% progression hurdle and 37.5pt clinical placement ensure demonstrated competence
- All subjects are compulsory — no dilution of specialist training
- AI is assisting variant interpretation but cannot conduct genetic counselling sessions
- Global demand for genetic counsellors is growing; Australian graduates can practice in NZ, UK, Canada, Singapore

**Confidence: MEDIUM-HIGH.** Genetic counselling is a structurally growing, highly regulated clinical profession.
