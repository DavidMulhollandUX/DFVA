## 4. DESTINATION CONVERGENCE & EXPOSURE READ

Destinations split between technology and quantitative employers (IBM, Canva, plus the consulting majors Accenture and Deloitte) and engineering firms (Beca, Jacobs). The junior-vs-senior bifurcation is the dominant dynamic: AI-assisted execution as a standalone junior task is compressing, while systems architecture, evaluation and direction are growing — exactly where the faculty's technical depth positions graduates.
