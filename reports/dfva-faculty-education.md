## 4. DESTINATION CONVERGENCE & EXPOSURE READ

Graduates flow to the Department of Education, the school systems, and universities, as classroom teachers, instructional designers, education officers and evaluators. These are relational, judgement-led roles with low automation exposure — but the EdTech and AI-assessment-design edge is where demand is moving and the curriculum lags.
