## 1. FACULTY PROFILE

Medicine, Dentistry and Health is the **highest-scoring and most consistent faculty**, anchored by a genuine physical-and-relational moat (maximal Domain Depth, Human & Relational, and Irreplaceability). Its one systemic weakness is the portfolio's lowest **AI Literacy** — a clinical-AI paradox: hands-on clinical work cannot be automated, yet AI diagnostic tools are entering routine practice and graduates have little framework to govern, evaluate or contextualise them. The risk here is reputational and regulatory, not displacement.

3. Preserve and make explicit the physical/relational moat in programme positioning — it is the portfolio's strongest genuine AI defence.
