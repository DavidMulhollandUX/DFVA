## 4. DESTINATION CONVERGENCE & EXPOSURE READ

Business and Economics is the origin of the **Big-Four consulting funnel**. Deloitte, PwC, KPMG and EY — plus the major banks (ANZ, NAB, Commonwealth, Macquarie) — are the dominant destinations, and the modal entry role is **Business Analyst**, alongside Graduate/Investment/Financial Analyst. This is the AI-substitution-pressure zone: junior analyst work (data analysis, reporting, research synthesis). The exposure is doubly signalled here — a low Automation-Exposure curriculum score AND a modal destination squarely in the exposed tier.
